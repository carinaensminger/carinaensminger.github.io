bio

I am a UX/UI Designer & Strategist working and living in Boston. I want to design and build products that matter - products that solve consumer pain points and make an impact in user's lives.

I love speaking to and understanding people. I believe that all products that make a difference begin with the user, begin with empathy and observation. This philosophy informs my design process and decisions.

When I'm not designing websites and apps, I'm reading, knitting, playing video games, or practicing circus trapeze.
