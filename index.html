about

Passionate, data-driven, and innovative UX | UI Designer, Strategist, and Thinker living in Boston.

I am a UX / UI Designer and Strategist hoping to join a creative, innovative, and passionate startup in Boston. I want to design and build products that matter - products that solve consumer pain points and make an impact in user's lives. My background is in marketing, volunteer recruiting, and fundraising.

I love speaking to and understanding people. I believe that all products that make a difference begin with the user, begin with the designer empathizing with users, understanding their needs, watching their behaviors, and designing solutions around those needs. This philosophy informs my design process and decisions.
