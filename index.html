I am a UX / UI Designer hoping to join a creative, innovative, and passionate startup in Boston. I want to design and build products that matter - products that solve consumer pain points and make an impact in user's lives.

I began my career at BUILD Greater Boston - a non-profit that supports high school students in starting and launching their own small businesses.During my time with BUILD, I discovered a passion for user experience and user interface design. To pursue this new line of work, I joined the Startup Institute as a Web Design student in the Spring 2016.

This rough framework outlines my design process:

  • Research: Understand the user and their needs.
  • Brainstorm: Brainstorm solutions that meet user needs.
  • Build: Build one or two prototypes of the best solutions. Wireframe and create high fidelity mockups.
  • Test: Put your prototype in front of users and test your solutions and assumptions.
  • Repeat: Use the findings of your user test to refine your solutions.
