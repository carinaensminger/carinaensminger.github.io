about me

bio

I am a UX/UI Designer & Strategist hoping to join a creative, innovative, and passionate design team in Boston. I want to design and build products that matter - products that solve consumer pain points and make an impact in user's lives.

I love speaking to and understanding people. I believe that all products that make a difference begin with the user, begin with the designer empathizing with users, understanding their needs, watching their behaviors, and designing solutions around those needs. This philosophy informs my design process and decisions.

When I'm not designing websites and mobile apps, I'm reading, knitting, playing video games, or practicing circus trapeze.
